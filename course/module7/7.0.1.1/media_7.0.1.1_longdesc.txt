Figure lists objectives for the chapter.  Upon completion of this chapter the learner will be able to describe the purpose of the transport layer in the transportation of data in end-to-end communication.  The learner will be able to describe the characteristics of the T.C.P. and U.D.P. protocols, including port numbers and their uses.  The learner will be able to explain how T.C.P. session establishment and termination processes facilitate reliable communication.  The learner will be able to determine whether high-reliability T.C.P. transmission or non-guaranteed U.D.P. transmissions are best suited for common applications.
