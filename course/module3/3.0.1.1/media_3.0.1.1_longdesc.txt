Figure lists objectives for chapter three.  Upon completion of this chapter the learner will be able to explain why I.T. protocols are necessary in communication; they will be able to explain the purpose of adhering to a protocol suite.  They will be able to explain how the T.C.P.I.P model and the O.S.I. model are used to facilitate standardization in the communication process.  The learner will be able to explain why R.F.C.s became the process for establishing standards.  They will be able describe the R.F.C. process.  They will be able to explain how local hosts access local resources on a network, and how local hosts access remote resources on a network.
